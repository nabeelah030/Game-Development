Game Design Document

The Attack

Executive Summary:-

In this game we have mainly two target Audiences. One is the Attackers and the other one is a person who is protecting from the Attacker. In this game the basic concept is that a person stuck in an area were there are a Attacker. He protects himself from the Attacker. There are 3 level, and each level is different and difficult from the previous level. So here a person protect himself from the Attacker and, he will be careful from the tripe which is for Attacker only there are some sort of gift come for help that person.

Game play:-

Game Concept
The main objective of this game is , if a person stuck in such a situation then what he will do to protect himself there are a lots of tripes and hurdle in this game
Game Progressions
We divide this game into three levels. Here each level is different and difficult from the previous level. Here in this game, we keep many hurdles and a player protect himself form Attacker and protect himself from those hurdles. There are some sort of gift come for help that person in these gift there are gun to kill the Attacker.

Rules:-
The rules of this game are so simple. A player will protect from any sort of hurdles and he has to kill all the Attacker in the level as required by the developer. In the first level we will kill 50 Attacker. In the second level we will kill 100 Attacker. In the third level we will kill 150 Attacker.
combat:-
This game is base on a single and individual player who protect himself from Attacker.
